About Us

We are a team hailing from different parts of Vietnam (some of us are not even living in Vietnam right now!). We have had many past professions: some of us were graphic designers, translators, IT developers, and coders, but we share the same goal! As the name of our company implies, we are Five Elements that are different with each other, but together, combine into the universe.

We aim to bring everyone together and offer a standard of service on-par with the best of the world.

We are passionate, confident, and willing to learn, and we believe everything will fall into places as we strive to do the best we can.
